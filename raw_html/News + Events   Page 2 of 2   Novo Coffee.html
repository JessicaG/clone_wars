Novo featured in Wynkoop Brewery’s Vonnegut Dinner

Along with our good friends at Tender Belly, Novo Coffee was the featured menu item at Wynkoop Brewery's "Breakfast of Champions" Vonnegut tribute dinner. Kurt's Mile High lager featured Novo Coffee in a receipe that changed hands between the legendary author and Governor Hickenlooper's father.  The 18-barrel batch served to celebrate both Vonnegut's life and the 25th anniversary of Wynkoop Brewery.

Cascara Quad: Novo Teams Up With New Belgium

Novo has teamed up with Fort Collins-based New Belgium brewery to make a coffee-cherry infused beer. Coffee cherries, known as cascara, is the fruit surrounding the coffee bean and is frequently used to make tea in countries where coffee trees grow. The Trappist ale, Cascara Quad, will be released as part of New Belgium's well known limited edition Lips of Faith series.
